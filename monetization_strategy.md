# MHS Loop Wiki: Monetization Strategy

The goal of the MHS Loop Wiki is to provide the highest-value, most practical travel guide for riders while generating sustainable passive income. This document outlines the primary and secondary revenue streams for the project.

## 💰 Core Revenue Streams

### 1. Affiliate Marketing (High Priority)
The most direct way to monetize the site without degrading the user experience. By recommending only the services we actually use and trust, we maintain credibility while earning commissions.

| Affiliate Partner | Product / Category | Implementation | Potential Commission |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stay22** | Accommodations | Map embeds in Town Guides & Itinerary pages. | ~5-10% of booking |
| **BNE SIM** | eSIM / Connectivity | Comparison table & deep-dive guide. | ~10-20% per sale |
| **Genki / SafetyWing** | Travel Insurance | Dedicated Insurance & Safety page. | ~$10-15 per lead |
| **Amazon Associates** | Gear & Packing | Links for Phone Mounts, Dry Bags, Gloves. | 4-10% of sale |

### 2. Bike Rental Partnerships (Mid Priority)
Since we are funneling high-intent travelers directly to shops like Mango Bikes or Mr Mechanic, we can leverage this for referral fees.
- **Direct Referrals:** Partner with 1-2 top-tier shops for a flat lead-gen fee (e.g., 100 THB per rental) for users who mention "MHS Loop Wiki".
- **BikesBooking.com:** An affiliate aggregator for motorcycle rentals.

### 3. Direct Brand Partnerships (Long Term)
As the site grows in authority (SEO ranking for "Mae Hong Son Loop"), we can approach specific high-end stays or tour operators for sponsored content.
- **Example:** A "Glamping Spotlight" feature for a specific resort in Ban Rak Thai.

## 📈 Projected Success Metrics

To reach a sustainable revenue of **$500 - $1,000 / month**, we aim for the following traffic targets:

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monthly Visitors** | 10,000+ | Focused, niche traffic with high intent to spend. |
| **Affiliate Click-Through** | 5-7% | Using high-value "Buy" buttons and interactive maps. |
| **Conversion Rate** | 2-3% | Industry standard for travel bookings. |
