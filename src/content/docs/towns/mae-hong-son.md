# 🏯 Mae Hong Son City Master Directory

Welcome to the heart of the loop. Mae Hong Son City is where the "adventure" turns into "culture." It’s quieter than Pai, more traditional, and centered around a stunning Burmese-style temple lake. 

**Our Goal:** We’ve cataloged every major stay in town so you don't have to guess. Use this list to pick your base for exploring the Long Neck villages or the climb to Doi Kong Mu.

## 📍 Don't Leave Without Seeing:
1.  **Wat Phra That Doi Kong Mu:** Ride the steep hill at sunset. It overlooks the entire valley.
2.  **The Night Market:** Every evening around the lake. The best street food in town.
3.  **Su Tong Pae Bridge:** A bamboo bridge over rice fields just North of the city.
