# 🛶 Mae Sariang Master Directory

Mae Sariang is the "zen" of the loop. It’s a peaceful, teak-wood town on the Yuam River that feels like Thailand did 30 years ago. No crowds, no stress—just great riverside views and some of the best rider-focused hospitality on the circuit.

**Our Goal:** Total coverage of every bed in town. Because this is the quietest stop, you can usually find a room on arrival, but these "Top Picks" fill up fast.

## 📍 Mae Sariang Bucket List:
1.  **Riverside Sunset:** Grab a Beer Lao and watch the sun dip behind the Burmese mountains.
2.  **Wat Jong Sung:** Beautiful Burmese-style temple in the center of town.
3.  **Salawin River Trip:** 45 mins west to the border. Rent a boat to see the Myanmar side.
