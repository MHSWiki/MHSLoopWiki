This itinerary covers the most scenic and practical stops for a 5-day motorcycle loop through Northern Thailand.

---

## 🗺️ The Route Summary
*   **Duration:** 5 Days
*   **Main Highlights:** 1,864 Curves + the Highest Peak in Thailand.
*   **Direction:** Counter-Clockwise (Pai first).

---

## 🗓️ Day-by-Day Journey

### Day 1: Chiang Mai → Pai
- **Mok Fa Waterfall:** Perfect morning dip (Km 20).
- **Pong Dueat Geyser:** Boiling geyser (150°C).
- **Sunset:** Pai Memorial Bridge & Pai Canyon.

### Day 2: The Pai Explorer
- **Santichon Village:** Traditional Chinese Yunnanese site.
- **Sunset:** The White Big Buddha overlooking the valley.
- **Night:** Pai Walking Street for local food.

### Day 3: Pai → Mae Hong Son
- **Lod Cave (Tham Lod):** One of the largest caves in Thailand.
- **Lakeside Village:** Ban Rak Thai for tea and views.
- **City Sunset:** Wat Phra That Doi Kong Mu.

### Day 4: Mae Hong Son → Mae Sariang
- **Mae Surin Waterfall:** A massive drop waterfall.
- **History:** Thai-Japan WWII Museum in Khun Yuam.
- **Base:** Rest in the river city of Mae Sariang.

### Day 5: Mae Sariang → Chiang Mai
- **Doi Inthanon:** Thailand’s highest point.
- **Waterfalls:** Wachiratan and the massive Mae Ya waterfall.

---

## 💡 Top Tips
- Download offline maps for sections between Pai and Mae Hong Son.
- Reach Ban Rak Thai before sunset for the best photos of the tea fields.
- Use low gear on the 11-km ascent to Doi Inthanon summit.
