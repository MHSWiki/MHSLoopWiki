# 📝 MHS Loop Wiki: Project Masterplan

This document outlines the strategic vision, audience, and monetization plan for the **1,864 Curve Masterplan**.

---

## 🎯 Site Objectives
1.  **The Gold Standard:** To be the most accurate and up-to-date guide for the Mae Hong Son Loop on the internet.
2.  **Radical Actionability:** Information must be scannable (less than 1 minute to read any section) and practical (no "fluff").
3.  **Community Hub:** Cultivate a community of "survivors" who tag and share their experience via #MHSWiki.
4.  **One-Page Authority:** Ensure the homepage acts as a "Site Map" or "Quick-Start" for riders who are literally standing in a parking lot looking for a bike.

---

## 🏍️ Target Audience
*   **The Primary Rider:** Backpackers and long-term travelers already in Chiang Mai or Pai.
*   **Experience Level:** Ranges from "never touched a scooter" to "adv touring pro."
*   **Mindset:** Adventurous, looking for a life-changing experience, but also cautious about hospital bills and bike scams.
*   **Tone Preference:** Friendly, conversational, expert-led. "I've done this, here is how you don't mess it up."

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
The goal is to provide value-first monetization that actually helps the traveler.

### 1. Accommodation Affiliates (Stay22)
*   Integrate interactive maps and "Top Pick" cards with **Stay22** links on every town page.
*   Focus on "Rider-Friendly" spots (Piya Guesthouse, Riverhouse, etc.).

### 2. Travel & Gear Affiliates (Travelpayouts)
*   **Insurance:** Direct links to SafetyWing or World Nomads for riders needing immediate cover.
*   **Gear:** Small affiliate links to essential gear (dry bags, phone mounts) available on Amazon or Shopee.

### 3. Local Partnerships (Lead Gen)
*   **Mango Bikes / Tony’s Big Bikes:** Negotiated referral codes or exclusive discounts for MHSWiki users.
*   **Aya Service:** Link to minivan bookings for those who want to "cheat" the Pai leg or send their bags ahead.
